# Voice assistant

When enabled, a microphone button appears in the bottom-right corner of every
page. Tap it and speak — the assistant can answer questions about the site and
take you where you want to go ("show me office chairs", "open my orders",
"go to the blog").

- The first tap asks your browser for microphone permission. If you decline, the
  button shows *Microphone needed*; allow it and tap again.
- While connected the button pulses and shows *Listening…* / *Speaking…*.
- The assistant can move you around the site as you talk — the conversation keeps
  going while the page changes.
- Tap the button again to end the conversation.

Your voice is streamed to the assistant only while a conversation is active.

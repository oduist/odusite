# Shopping

## Catalog

Browse `/shop`: filter by category, attributes and price, search by name, and
sort. Product pages show variants (color, size, …) — pick options to see the
exact price and availability.

## Cart

The cart keeps its contents for 90 days on your device — no account needed.
Signing in during checkout attaches the cart to your account.

## Checkout

1. **Address** — billing address, plus a separate delivery address if needed.
2. **Delivery** — choose among available delivery methods; the total updates
   immediately.
3. **Payment** — pay by card (or other offered methods) right on the page.
   Card data is handled by the payment provider (e.g. Stripe); the shop never
   sees your card number.

After payment you land on the confirmation page with your order number. A
confirmation email follows. The order appears in **Portal → Orders** if you
have an account.

## Quotations

If the company sent you a quotation, open it from the email link or
**Portal → Quotations**: review, **sign online** (draw your signature),
**decline** with a comment, or **pay** when payment is required to confirm.

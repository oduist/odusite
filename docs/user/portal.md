# Portal — your documents

Open **Portal** from the header after signing in. The home page shows what's
available to you; sections appear only when relevant.

## Orders & quotations

Lists of your confirmed orders and open quotations with status, totals and
detail pages: line items, delivery address, linked invoices, and a PDF
download. Quotations can be signed, declined or paid online. Each document has
a message thread — write there to reach the company; replies arrive by email
too.

## Invoices

Your invoices with payment status (paid / open / overdue). Open an invoice for
details, download the official PDF, or **pay online** when a payment provider
is configured.

## Projects & tasks

If the company shares projects with you: browse your projects, see tasks with
their stage and deadline, read descriptions and attachments, and discuss in
the task's message thread.

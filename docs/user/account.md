# Your account

## Signing in

Use **Sign in** in the header. Your credentials are the same ones you received
by email when the company granted you portal access.

## Creating an account

If self-registration is enabled, **Create an account** (from the sign-in page)
registers you with your name, email and a password. For security we then email
you a **confirmation link** — click it to activate your account, then sign in.
Until you confirm, signing in shows a reminder with a **Resend confirmation
email** button (also available on the sign-up page). Confirmation links expire
after 48 hours; just resend if yours has.

Forgot your password? Use the reset link on the sign-in page — you'll receive
an email with a link back to the site to set a new one.

## Account settings (Portal → Account)

- **Profile & billing address** — your name, email, phone and address. Some
  fields (e.g. company, VAT) may be locked once documents exist; contact the
  company to change them.
- **Addresses** — additional billing/delivery addresses used at checkout.
- **Security** — change your password (this signs out all other devices) and
  review active sessions with the option to revoke any of them.

## Privacy

You stay signed in for up to 30 days on a device unless you sign out or revoke
the session. Signing out on one device does not affect others — use
*Security → revoke* for that.

# Events, jobs, forum, courses

## Events

`/events` lists upcoming events with dates in the event's timezone. On an
event page pick tickets and register attendees — you'll get a confirmation
email and a calendar (ICS) link. Paid tickets, where offered, are paid at
registration.

## Jobs

`/jobs` lists open positions with filters by department, location and type.
Apply on the job page with your contact details and CV; you'll be contacted by
the recruiting team. Applying twice to the same position is blocked while your
first application is in progress.

## Forum

Read questions freely. To ask, answer, comment or vote, sign in first. Voting
and moderation follow karma rules — some actions unlock as your karma grows.
Accepted answers are highlighted; your profile page shows karma and badges.

## Courses

`/courses` lists available courses. Preview lessons are open to everyone;
join a course (sign-in required) to unlock the full curriculum, track your
progress, and take quizzes. Your completion percentage is shown on the course
page.
